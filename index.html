About Jem

Jem, also known as Jerrica Benton, is the lead singer of the sensational band Jem and the Holograms. With her incredible musical talent, charismatic stage presence, and the help of Synergy, a powerful holographic computer, Jem leads her band to stardom while battling the villainous Misfits.

Her story is one of friendship, music, and the power of believing in yourself. Jerrica's dual identity as the shy, compassionate businesswoman and the glamorous, rock-star Jem highlights themes of inner strength and transformation.

Synergy, created by Jerrica's father, Emmett Benton, is the source of Jem's holographic abilities, allowing her to project dazzling illusions and create truly outrageous performances.
