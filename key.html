About Jem

Jem, also known as Jerrica Benton, is the lead singer of the sensational band Jem and the Holograms. With her incredible musical talent, charismatic stage presence, and the help of Synergy, a powerful holographic computer, Jem leads her band to stardom while battling the villainous Misfits.

Her story is one of friendship, music, and the power of believing in yourself. Jerrica's dual identity as the shy, compassionate businesswoman and the glamorous, rock-star Jem highlights themes of inner strength and transformation.

Synergy, created by Jerrica's father, Emmett Benton, is the source of Jem's holographic abilities, allowing her to project dazzling illusions and create truly outrageous performances.

Meet the Band
"Showtime, Synergy!"

The Holograms

Jem (Jerrica Benton)

The lead singer and heart of the band. Jerrica transforms into Jem with the help of Synergy, bringing music and magic to the world. She's compassionate, determined, and truly outrageous.

Kimber Benton

Jerrica's younger sister, Kimber is the keyboardist and songwriter for The Holograms. She's energetic, sometimes impulsive, and always ready for adventure.

Aja Leith

The cool and collected lead guitarist. Aja is a tech whiz and a loyal friend, always there to back up Jem with her shredding guitar riffs and quick thinking.

Shana Elmsford

The drummer and fashion designer of the group. Shana is artistic, supportive, and brings a unique style to the band's look and sound.

Raya Alonso

The newest addition to the band, Raya is a talented percussionist who brings a vibrant energy and diverse rhythms to The Holograms' sound.

The Misfits

Every hero needs a rival, and Jem's are The Misfits! Led by the fiery Pizzazz, this punk-rock band is always trying to outshine Jem and the Holograms, often resorting to outrageous schemes.

Pizzazz (Phyllis Gabor)

The arrogant and ambitious leader of The Misfits. Pizzazz is a powerful vocalist with a fierce personality, constantly scheming to sabotage Jem.

Roxy (Roxanne Pellegrini)

The tough and often dim-witted guitarist of The Misfits. Roxy is the muscle of the group, always ready for a fight or to cause trouble.

Stormer (Mary Phillips)

The kind-hearted and often naive keyboardist of The Misfits. Stormer is the only member who sometimes shows remorse or kindness, making her the most conflicted.

Jetta (Sheila Burns)

The saxophone player and "British" member of The Misfits, Jetta is a master manipulator and often helps Pizzazz concoct her schemes.
